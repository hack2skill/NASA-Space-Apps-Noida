MORE FATCS TO KNOW

  1. Atmosphere: Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide (about 95.3%), with trace amounts of nitrogen, argon, and other gases.
  2. Water on Mars: There is evidence of liquid water in Mars' distant past, and it is believed that Mars once had rivers, lakes, and possibly an ocean.
  3. Future Exploration: Mars continues to be a major focus of planetary science and exploration, with ongoing and planned missions to study its geology, climate, and potential habitability.
