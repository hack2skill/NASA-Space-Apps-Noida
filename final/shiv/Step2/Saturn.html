FACTS TO KNOW

  1. Temperature: Saturn is an extremely cold planet in its upper atmosphere, with temperatures dropping as low as -178 degrees Celsius
  2. At the core, it is estimated to be around 11,700 degrees Celsius (21,000 degrees Fahrenheit).
  3. Physical Characteristics: Size and Composition: Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is the second-largest planet in our solar system, after Jupiter.
  4. Cassini-Huygens Mission: NASA's Cassini spacecraft, in collaboration with the European Space Agency (ESA) and the Italian Space Agency (ASI), conducted an extensive mission to study Saturn and its moons.
