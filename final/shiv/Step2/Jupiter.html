MORE FACTS TO KNOW

  1. Atmosphere: Jupiter's atmosphere is primarily composed of hydrogen (about 75%) and helium (about 24%), with trace amounts of other gases like methane and ammonia.
  2. Exploration: Several spacecraft have been sent to study Jupiter, including NASA's Juno mission, which has been studying the planet since 2016.
  3. Distance The distance between Jupiter and Earth varies because both planets have elliptical orbits around the Sun.
  4. On average, the closest approach or opposition of Jupiter to Earth occurs approximately every 13 months.
