FACTS TO KNOW

  1. Blue Planet: Earth is often referred to as the "Blue Planet" because of the prominent blue appearance of its oceans when seen from space.
  2. Landforms: From space, you can see various landforms, including continents, mountain ranges, deserts, and forests.
  3. Some of the most recognizable features include the Amazon Rainforest, the Himalayan mountain range, and the Sahara Desert.
  4. Atmosphere: Earth's atmosphere is a thin layer of gases that envelopes the planet. It appears as a hazy blue ring when viewed from space.
  5. City Lights: At night, the Earth's surface is dotted with the bright lights of cities and towns.
