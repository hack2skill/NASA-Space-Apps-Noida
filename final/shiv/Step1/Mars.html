Mars

FATCS TO KNOW

  1. Average Distance from Sun is 93 miles
  2. Named after the Roman God of war
  3. Mars is the fourth planet as well as the second smallest planet in the solar system.
  4. Mars is titled as a red planet due to the iron oxide (rust) present in its rocks and soil.
  5. It can get pretty cold on Mars, temperatures can reach 20°C, but at its poles they can plummet to as low as -140°C.
