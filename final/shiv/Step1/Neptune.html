NEPTUNE

FATCS TO KNOW

  1. Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun, making it the most distant in the solar system.
  2. It would have been closer to the Sun before migrating out to its current position
  3. Neptune is named after the Roman god of the sea.
  4. Neptune has 6 faint rings.
  5. Despite being smaller than Uranus, Neptune has a greater mass.
