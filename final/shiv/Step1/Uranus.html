URANUS

FACTS TO KNOW

  1. Uranus is not visible to the naked eye even though it is the third-largest planet in our solar system
  2. Uranus is named after the Greek god of the sky, Ouranos or Uranus.
  3. The tilt of Uranus(23 degree.) may have been caused by a collision.
  4. Uranus is icy and burning hot, with extreme seasons.
